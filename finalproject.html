Final Project Proposal : LumoSense!



Project Concept

I want to build a motion sensor- triggered light that turns on when I walk into my room. I also want it to also slowly rotate and turn on and off by touch.



Anticipated Bill of Materials:

RGB LED strips (for a light source) Stepper motor (for rotation) Assorted resistors (for current-limiting) Microcontroller (Arduino Uno) Breadboard 3d printed base PIR sensor (motion sensing) Capacitive sensor (touch sensing) Power supply Transistor Yarn/beads for a decorative shade



Timeline:

1. Draw schematic 2. Gather materials 3. Electronic components A. Get sensors working (capacitive + motion) B. Get output working (lighting + stepper motor) 4. 3d print base to house components 5. Connect base to lights and stepper motor



Contingency Plan

I anticipate that the rotation will be the most difficult aspect of this project. I will leave that part out if it is too difficult to implement, or add a different rotating piece I am also considering using fairy lights rather than LED strips, which may require additional components
